About UsB.R. Metal & Gen. Set

Established in 1990, B.R. Metal & Gen. Set has been a trusted name in power solutions and industrial scrap trading for over 30+ years.

Under the leadership of Mr. Rajkumar (Raju), we specialize in Generators, Motors, Inverters, and Electrical Equipment. We provide complete solutions including sales, purchase, installation, and maintenance services.

We also deal in industrial machinery scrap and are actively involved in the sale & purchase of Copper, Brass, Aluminium, and other metal scrap. Our goal is to deliver reliable products and fair market value to our customers.

With years of experience and a strong network, we ensure high-quality service, durable equipment, and customer satisfaction in every project we handle.
