The Package comes in Fully Loaded version (i.e., CAMC) which includes agreed number of Preventive maintenance services and includes almost all parts (including but not restricted to consumables, e.g., filters, oil, hoses, belts etc) as well as unlimited breakdown services.

  • Comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (CAMC)

  • Flexible plans to suit operational needs

Why Choose AMC? Maximize uptime, predictable costs, expert service visits, and priority support.

Proactive Maintenance Service

PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE

Preventive maintenance helps in avoiding sudden failures, reducing repair costs, and increasing the life of your equipment. Our experienced technicians perform detailed inspections and servicing of generators, motors, and electrical systems to keep them in top condition.

We follow a complete maintenance checklist including engine inspection, oil and filter change, cooling system check, fuel system cleaning, and electrical testing to ensure maximum efficiency and performance.

  • Regular servicing based on usage and running hours
  • Complete inspection and health check report
  • Use of genuine spare parts, oil, and filters
  • Quick response for emergency breakdown service
  • Maintenance support for generators, motors, and inverters

Why Preventive Maintenance is Important:
✔ Avoid unexpected power failure
✔ Increase equipment life
✔ Reduce repair and fuel costs
✔ Ensure safety and smooth performance

Our goal is to provide reliable, cost-effective, and long-term maintenance solutions so your business never stops due to power issues.
